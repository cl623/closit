Product Summary: clos.it
Concept:
clos.it is a gamified, social mood-boarding platform where users style 2D avatars using community-generated fashion items. It merges the visual inspiration of Pinterest with the interactive creativity of digital dress-up communities.

Target Audience:
Gen Z and Millennial digital artists, fashion enthusiasts, and users who enjoy avatar-based communities and aesthetic mood-boarding.

Core Value Proposition:

For Users: A creative sandbox to design, upload, and style digital outfits, earn social influence through leaderboards, and discover new aesthetics.

For the Business: A proprietary data engine that tracks granular, real-time fashion trends, item pairings, and aesthetic shifts based on active user behavior.

Key Mechanics:

Creation & Curation: Users start with a base 2D avatar and layer imported or drawn items (shirts, accessories, hair) onto a visual canvas.

Granular Social Engagement: Outfits are published to a feed. Viewers can "like" the overall look, or drill down to like and save individual items from that look to their own digital closets.

Gamified Influence: Engagement metrics (likes, shares, saves) power monthly stylistic leaderboards, awarding badges and status to top creators and trendsetters.
